Bus travel report that shows mean travel time by number of stops, and average travel time by reasson?

I am analyzing to see if the average travel time is affected by the number of stops it makes, and if different reasons affects the travel time.

This data was collected mainly by me, however my brother and friends helped me collect data by going on the bus and filling out the survey.

This will update as 20 people record their bus journey time from Stop C to Stockholm Road. If more people do the Google Form, this number will increase for each respondent.

From the collected data, we can see that the average bus travel time was 60.4 minutes, with the longest trip taking 75 minutes.

On average, buses made 23.1 stops per trip, with a maximum of 36 stops.

This suggest that the more bus stops there are, the longer the bus takes from stop c, to Stockholm road.

The plot for travel reasons also hint that delays massively impacts the travel time compared to the other reasons. Traffic is then the 2 highest reason that increases travel time which is slightly higher compared to passengers boarding and leaving.

In conclusion the most significant reason that affects travel time is delays, followed up with traffic and then passengers.
